Evening: Santa Fe Opera - Madama Butterfly

8:00 PM - We know the wedding ends on Friday, but we'd love for everyone to stick around and join us for the final performance of the opera season!

Joan and Fred used to take the entire Hart family to the Santa Fe Opera every year, with dinner at Bishop's Lodge beforehand. And Madama Butterfly holds special meaning for the Wessen Chang family. It feels right to close out the weekend together at this magical place.

The open-air opera house has stunning views of the sunset over the Jemez Mountains - an unforgettable experience.
