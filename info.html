Guide to Santa Fe

Travel

By Air

The closest airport is Santa Fe Regional Airport (SAF), about 15 minutes from Bishop's Lodge.

For more flight options, fly into Albuquerque International Sunport (ABQ), about 1 hour south. The Sunport is lovely, and gives you an opportunity to stop and eat at the Frontier before heading up to Santa Fe. You'll likely find more flight options flying into ABQ than SAF. The drive up is beautiful!

By Car

Santa Fe is easily accessible via I-25. Bishop's Lodge is just 10 minutes north of the historic Plaza, nestled in the foothills of the Sangre de Cristo Mountains.
