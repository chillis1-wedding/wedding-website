Guide to Santa Fe

Travel

By Air

The closest airport is Santa Fe Regional Airport (SAF), about 15 minutes from Bishop's Lodge.

For more flight options, fly into Albuquerque International Sunport (ABQ), about 1 hour south. The drive up is beautiful!

By Car

Santa Fe is easily accessible via I-25. Bishop's Lodge is just 10 minutes north of the historic Plaza, nestled in the foothills of the Sangre de Cristo Mountains.

Accommodations

Due to limited availability, we recommend booking accommodations as early as possible.

Bishop's Lodge $$$

Stay at the venue! Bishop's Lodge is a stunning 317-acre resort with casitas, suites, a spa, pool, and incredible mountain views.

Visit Bishop's Lodge Website

An exquisite boutique hotel with stunning Tibetan and Southwestern design. A truly unique Santa Fe experience.

A Santa Fe landmark right on the historic Plaza. Beautiful Southwestern architecture, rooftop bar, and steps from great restaurants and shops.
