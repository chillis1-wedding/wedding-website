Getting There

By Air

The closest airport is Santa Fe Regional Airport (SAF), about 15 minutes from Bishop's Lodge.

For more flight options, fly into Albuquerque International Sunport (ABQ), about 1 hour south. The drive up is beautiful!

By Car

Santa Fe is easily accessible via I-25. Bishop's Lodge is just 10 minutes north of the historic Plaza, nestled in the foothills of the Sangre de Cristo Mountains.

What to Wear

Dress Code

Black Tie Optional, Cowboy Flair Encouraged

Dress to impress, but don't be afraid to add some Southwest style! August evenings in Santa Fe are beautiful - warm days and cool nights. The ceremony and reception will be outdoors, so consider:

  • Elegant gowns or cocktail dresses
  • Tuxedos, suits, or a sharp western-cut jacket
  • Bolo ties, turquoise jewelry, cowboy boots - all welcome!
  • A light layer for the evening (it cools down in the mountains)

Where to Eat

Santa Fe has incredible food! A few of our favorites:

  • Cafe Pasqual's - Famous breakfast spot, get there early!
  • The Shed - Classic New Mexican, try the red chile
  • Geronimo - Upscale dining on Canyon Road
  • Iconik Coffee Roasters - Excellent local coffee with multiple locations
  • Modern General Feed and Seed - Hip cafe with great food and drinks in the Railyard
  • Kakawa Chocolate House - Historic drinking chocolates
  • Santa Fe Brewing Co. - Local craft beer in the Railyard
